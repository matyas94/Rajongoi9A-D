Viktor's Friends

Lőrinc Mészáros

Lőrinc Mészáros (Székesfehérvár, February 24, 1966) is a Hungarian entrepreneur who served as the Mayor of Felcsút from 2011 to 2018 and is the president of the Puskás Academy football club.

Vladimir Putin

Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin (Leningrad, October 7, 1952) is a former KGB agent, politician, and the President of Russia, serving as the country's top leader since 1999, holding continuous positions as president or prime minister.

Donald Trump

Donald John Trump (New York, June 14, 1946) is an American businessman, billionaire, and politician; he served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021.

Xi Jinping

Xi Jinping (Fuping or Beijing, June 15, 1953) is a Chinese politician serving as the paramount leader of China since 2012, and the General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) since 2012, as well as the chairman of the Central Military Commission (CMC) since 2012.

Recep Tayyip Erdoğan

Recep Tayyip Erdoğan (Istanbul, February 26, 1954) is an Islamist Turkish politician who served as the Prime Minister of Turkey from 2003 to 2014 and has been the President of Turkey since August 28, 2014.
